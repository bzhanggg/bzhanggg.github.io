About me


I am a student at Rutgers University studying mathematics and computer science. I’m currently a research assistant in the Rutgers Department of Computer Science, advised by Dr. Srinivas Narayana, exploring compiler optimizations for parallelized network packet processing in the Linux kernel using eBPF.

Previously, I was a research assistant under Dr. Farzad Yousefian, where I worked on distributed optimization frameworks for decentralized ML and game theory problems.

I’m excited to continue learning more about compilers, formal verification, and parallelization.
